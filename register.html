Electronic Voter Registration Application

Welcome to the Atlas Polls Electronic Voter Registration Application website.

You may use this application to submit an application for voter registration or to submit an update to your registration information.

For any election, the last day to register to vote or update your voter registration is the 15th day prior to the election.

To submit an application to register to vote, you must meet the following requirements:

  • You must be a student of Aptech Computer Institute.
  • You must live in the State of Imo.
  • You must be at least 10 years of age on or before election day.
  • You must not be barred from voting by reason of a disqualifying felony conviction.
  • You must not have been judged "mentally incompetent" or suspended by Aptech Computer Education

You will be required to Upload valid photo identification when you vote, unless exempted by law.

For questions regarding the requirements listed above and your eligibility, please Contact Us.

Do you currently have a Student Identification Card issued by Aptech Computer Education

In addition to the requirements listed above, you must have a valid Student Identification Card issued by Aptech Computer Education to submit an Electronic Voter Registration Application.
